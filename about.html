About Autohaus

Over 25 years of trusted automotive repair service. Family-owned, community-focused, and committed to excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Autohaus Collision Center Inc. started as a small family business with a big dream: to provide honest, high-quality auto body repair services to our local community.

Over the past 25+ years, we've grown from a two-bay operation to a state-of-the-art facility, but our core values remain unchanged. We treat every vehicle as if it were our own and every customer like family.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted collision repair centers in the area, with thousands of satisfied customers and a reputation built on quality workmanship and integrity.
